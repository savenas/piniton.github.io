Benefits

Free

It is a free application that anyone can download while traveling in Lithuania and have free access to full functionality. Available both on IOS and Android.

Progress Tracking

Our App will help you keep track of your exploration progress in a fun way!

Explore and Learn

You can trust our application to find the essential information on each object. Additionally, familiar objects will have optional short quizzes to help you make the most from exploring as well as help you earn more points!

Achievements

Pin it On will reward users with points for each visited location, in-game titles, and much more. Moreover, you will be greeted with weekly quests and unique events to keep you on the move!

Competition

To make it more interesting, you'll be able to compete with family, friends, and other users. Want to share it with others? No problem! You'll be able to do so via well known and already existing social media channels.

Suggestions made for you

For a small fee, Pin it On will help you plan your routes and get to know Lithuania and it's culture. You can rest assured that these routes will include events of all scales to make the most of your time.

Augmented reality

Every place is special. Let us help you catch the moment with a small twist - augmented reality. For each picture you take, based on your geographic location, we will have a special filter ready for you, which will enhance the image with the spirit of local culture.
